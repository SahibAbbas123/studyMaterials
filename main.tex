\textbf{Submitted by: Tarulata} \\
\textbf{Date:} 02/06/2025

\section{Introduction}

Tarulata is delighted to present this proposal for designing, installing, and maintaining a vibrant indoor and outdoor garden project at Chittagong Independent University (CIU). This green initiative aims to transform the university’s environment into a space that fosters freshness, beauty, and well-being for students, faculty, and visitors alike.

Our plan includes setting up \textbf{129 indoor plants} across two buildings and \textbf{107 outdoor plants} strategically placed around the Business School entrance and surroundings. This thoughtful combination of greenery will help CIU stand out as a modern, eco-conscious institution that values nature, wellness, and aesthetics.

\section{�� Post-Delivery Maintenance Terms \& Conditions}

\subsection*{Monthly Maintenance Fee}
After the successful delivery and setup of the plants, a monthly maintenance charge of \textbf{6,000 BDT} will apply. This fee covers the complete care and upkeep of the installed plants.

\subsection*{Gardener Service}
A dedicated gardener will visit \textbf{8 times per month} to ensure:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Proper watering
  \item Cleaning and pruning
  \item Pest and disease inspection
  \item Overall plant health and presentation
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Plant Replacement Policy}
If any plant requires replacement due to natural causes or environmental factors:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Tarulata will notify the authority in advance.
  \item Only the \textbf{actual cost} of the replaced plant(s) will be billed.
  \item Replacement will proceed \textbf{only after the client’s confirmation}.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Fertilizer and Medication}
Should additional fertilizers or plant medications be required:
\begin{itemize}
  \item They will be supplied with prior approval from the authority.
  \item The associated cost will be billed separately.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Commitment to Plant Longevity}
With routine maintenance and expert care, Tarulata ensures:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Long-term survival of all plants
  \item Healthy, sustainable growth
  \item An aesthetically pleasing environment
\end{itemize}
